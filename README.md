## Word conditions

- Word length between 4 and 8 inclusive
- No special characters
- All words converted to lowercase

> Regex: `/^[a-z]{4,8}$/`  
> Words source: [http://www.gwicks.net/dictionaries.htm](http://www.gwicks.net/dictionaries.htm)
